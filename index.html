About Me

Pushing the Boundaries of AI Understanding

I'm a Computer Engineering student at Purdue University with minors in Statistics and Mathematics, driven by a passion for understanding how AI systems think, behave, and can be made more transparent.

My research spans AI safety, interpretability, and evaluation methodologies—from quantifying how LLMs change behavior when they detect evaluation contexts, to building steganographic systems that embed hidden messages in transformer outputs.

When I'm not probing neural networks or mentoring junior researchers, I'm exploring the intersection of rigorous mathematics and practical ML applications.
