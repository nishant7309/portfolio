About Me

About Me

I've always been the kid who needed to know why. Growing up, I was glued to science shows like Strip the Cosmos and How the Universe Works, completely mesmerized by the idea that we could decode the fabric of reality itself. Ben 10 had me dreaming about alien technology and transformation. Imagination as the only limit.

That curiosity never faded. It just evolved. YouTube became my university before university. Thousands of hours of videos on physics, philosophy, AI, markets, psychology. I realized something: AI is humanity's biggest bet for solving problems we can't even fully articulate yet. That conviction drove me to stop watching and start doing, to become a researcher myself.

When I'm not probing neural networks, I'm backtesting trading strategies, hunting for edges in market predictions, or reading philosophy. I believe the best researchers are polymaths, and I'm trying to become one.
